Battle Of The Bands

Be it Pop, Rock, Jazz, put together your best singers and instrumentalists to come and compete in Battle of The Bands. This competition will test the ingenuity and skill of your group. Steer clear of elimination to be able to perform on the final day in front of a crowd.

Solo Singing

This is your chance to have your The Voice moment at our own singing competition! You have two minutes to wow the judges. A-capella, with a backing track, or while playing your own instrument. Participants who are qualified move to the second round which includes collaboration, synching and much more.

Mimicry Drama

Re-enact your favorite Bollywood movie scenes with a twist. In the “Wheel of Musical Impression'' style event, spin the wheel to match a famous Bollywood scene with a random mood and prepare a skit in less than two hours. You may become a sad “Poo” from K3G, or a haunted Anjali from KKHH!

Four Grids Comedy

Each spot on a grid of 4 will be given a specific character. Create a quick scene with 3 fellow contestants restricted in this box playing everyday characters like your local vegetable vendor. Your wit, chemistry and improv skills will be put to the test.

Dance Battle

The ultimate battle to know who can grove the best. Prepare your 3 minute choreography based on the themes given prior to the event through classical, contemporary, hip-hop or any style of your choice.
