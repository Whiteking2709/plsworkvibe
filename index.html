What will you find at Vibe?

"VIBE" is an inter-school event organized by NEXT School for students in grades 9-12. After the successful debut last year with the participation of over 350 enthusiastic students from various IB schools in Mumbai, we are committed to making this year's event even more impressive and educational.

VIBE goes beyond just being a fun event; our objective is to immerse students in real-world experiences that align with NEXT School's Big Picture Education system. We strongly believe that the most effective way to prepare students for the future is by giving them hands-on experiences. Therefore, each of our events is carefully crafted to include elements that challenge and enhance skills relevant to their future.


There are multiple fields like music, art, performance, sports, product design, trivia, fashion, cooking, and gaming. Within each of these areas, there are events tailored to various talents and abilities, providing a wide array of opportunities for participants. Moreover, we are excited to provide workshops associated with these domains to enrich the overall experience.
